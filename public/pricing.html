Frequently asked

When do submission limits reset?
On the first day of every calendar month (UTC). Limits are forward-looking — you start fresh each month.
What counts as a "partner link"?
Each unique link you create for a sending partner or sourcing channel. Disable a link to free up a slot — only active links count toward your limit.
What happens if I hit my limit?
Workers will see a friendly message asking them to reach out directly or try again next month. Your existing submissions and partner links stay accessible — only new submissions are paused until you upgrade or the month resets.
How do I upgrade?
Email upgrade@jobready.sg with your agency name and chosen plan. We'll activate it within one business day.
Can I downgrade later?
Yes — drop us an email any time. Your data stays intact; only the limits change.
