Privacy Policy

This page explains what JobReady collects, why it is collected, where submission files are stored, and how applicants can request changes or deletion.

Effective January 1, 2025
Last updated April 16, 2026

JobReady helps foreign workers prepare submissions for manpower agencies in Singapore. It captures the applicant data, generates the PDF, stores the result locally, and mirrors files to cloud storage when configured.

Scope

This policy covers information submitted through the JobReady website, including resume details, uploaded documents, generated files, and related storage paths.

1 Who We Are

JobReady is operated as a resume-building service connecting foreign workers with manpower agencies in Singapore. By using JobReady, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2 Information We Collect

When you use JobReady, we collect the information you provide during the form flow, which may include:

  • Full name and date of birth
  • Nationality and current location (country and city)
  • Phone number and email address
  • Work experience, job titles, and company names
  • Skills, certifications, and education level
  • Preferred job type, work arrangement, and expected salary
  • Languages spoken
  • The manpower agency selected at the start of the form
  • A generated submission ID used to track the resume request
  • Uploaded supporting documents that the applicant adds before submission

3 How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To generate your resume as a PDF document
  • To submit your resume to the manpower agency you specify
  • To store your resume, generated PDF, and supporting files so they can be reviewed and forwarded
  • To keep a local and mirrored submission record when storage mirroring is configured

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose beyond resume submission and handling.

4 How We Store Your Information

JobReady writes generated resumes and attachment bundles to the local project output structure under the selected agency folder. When Supabase credentials are configured, the same relative file structure is mirrored to cloud storage.

Access is restricted to authorised personnel only. Your data is not submitted through third-party messaging platforms from this website.

5 Who We Share Your Information With

Your information is shared only with:

  • The manpower agency you specify at the start of the form, for employment matching purposes
  • JobReady's operator, who reviews submissions and forwards resumes to agencies
  • Configured infrastructure providers that store the generated files and resume data on JobReady's behalf

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for commercial purposes.

6 Data Retention

Your resume and personal information are retained for as long as necessary to facilitate your job application and related review. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by stopping use of the service

8 Third-Party Services

JobReady uses Supabase to store resume data and generated files when the mirror is enabled. Browser-side OCR for resume import runs in the user's browser on the latest build. If additional services are added in the future, those services may have their own privacy policies.

9 Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us:

JobReady Operator

Singapore

Contact us via the JobReady website or support channel

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of JobReady after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
