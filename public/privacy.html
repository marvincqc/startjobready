Privacy Policy

This page explains what information JobReady collects when you fill in the application form, how it is used, and who it is shared with.

Effective 1 Jan 2025 Last updated 16 Apr 2026

1 Who We Are

JobReady is a job application service that helps workers apply to manpower agencies in Singapore. By using JobReady, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2 What We Collect

When you fill in the application form, we collect the information you provide, which may include:

  • Full name and date of birth
  • Nationality and current location (country and city)
  • Phone number and email address
  • Work experience, job titles, and company names
  • Skills, certifications, and education level
  • Preferred job type, work arrangement, and expected salary
  • Languages spoken
  • The manpower agency you are applying to
  • Any supporting documents you upload (passport, certificates, etc.)

If you arrived through a partner agency link, we also record which partner agency referred you and a code that identifies that link.

3 How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to process your job application:

  • To generate your resume as a PDF document
  • To send your application to the manpower agency you chose
  • To keep your submission tagged to the correct agency and referral partner
  • To store your resume and uploaded files so the agency can review and forward them

We do not use your information for marketing or advertising.

4 How We Store Your Information

Your application — including the generated resume PDF and any files you upload — is saved securely on our servers, organised by the agency you applied to. It is also backed up to cloud storage.

Access is restricted to authorised personnel only. Your data is not submitted through third-party messaging platforms from this website.

5 Who We Share Your Information With

Your information is shared only with:

  • The manpower agency you selected, for employment matching purposes
  • The overseas partner or referral agency that sent you the application link, when needed to coordinate your application handoff
  • JobReady's operator, who manages submissions and forwards resumes to agencies

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for commercial purposes.

6 How Long We Keep It

Your resume and personal information are kept for as long as needed to process your job application. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • See the personal information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct any information that is wrong
  • Ask us to delete your data
  • Stop using the service at any time

8 Third-Party Services

JobReady uses secure cloud storage to back up your resume and uploaded files. Browser-side OCR (used to scan an existing resume image) runs entirely in your browser — nothing is sent to a third party during that step.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be shown on this page with an updated date. Continuing to use JobReady after changes means you accept the updated policy.
